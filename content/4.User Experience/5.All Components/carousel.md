# Carousel

![Carousel](/img/Carousel.png)

**Use case**<br>
When you want to share slides of multiple images on one page.

**Limitations**<br>
There must be 3 to 10 slides.

**What’s required?**<br>
Each slide must have an image and caption.

**What’s optional?**<br>
Section heading, introduction
